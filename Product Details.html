About Product

Technical Content: Thiamethoxam 25 % WG

Actara Insecticide is second generation neonicotinoid for controlling foliar and soil pests
in many crops at low use rates, in a variety of application types such as Soil applications,
seed treatments and foliar sprays.

Targeted Pests: Aphids, Ash weevil, Black aphids, Brown plant leafhopper, Bugs,
Cardamom aphid, Chilli thrips, Fruit rust thrips, Grape thrips, hispa, Jassids,
Mango hoppers, Marginal gall thrips, Pod fly, Rice hispa, Rhizome weevil,
Spiraling whitefly, sugarcane wolly aphid, White flies, White tail mealy bug, Mealy bugs,
Anar butterfly may be managed by spraying Actara

Dosage: 0.5 gm/litre of water and 100 gm in 200 liters of water per acre
